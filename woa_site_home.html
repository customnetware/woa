About Woodbridge

The construction of Woodbridge began in 2006 and was completed in 2017. The community consists of 1,425 single-family ranch homes. All of these homes are available for resale only. The homes come in 14 floor plans from 1,312-2,618 square feet, with either 2 or 3 bedrooms, 2 or 2.5 bathrooms, and an attached 2 to 3-car garage. Del Webb's signature big rooms and open layouts make these homes perfect for active adults looking for a low-maintenance, charming home in Manteca.

Woodbridge Active Adult community is situated in a desirable area with a variety of recreational activities nearby. The area is home to a variety of golf courses, both private and public, and is the site of one of California's largest bowling centers. The region's regional parks provide a variety of fishing and boating opportunities in the Delta waterways, as well as RV and tent camping, picnic areas, barbecue pits, horseshoes pits, and child's play areas, making these parks ideal and cost-effective options for any time of year. The City is also home to Bass Pro Shops and BIg League Dreams. Woodbridge is also just a two-hour drive from the Bay Area, and it's only an hour drive to Sacramento and an hour to San Jose, where there's plenty of fun things to do, places to visit, and shopping to be done..
Mission Statement

The Woodbridge Owners Association safeguards the common interests and physical assets of the association that afford members an enhanced and rewarding quality of life in a fiscally responsible manner.

Our vision is to create a proud, welcoming, and desirable 55+ Active Adult Community with a friendly and enjoyable lifestyle for its members, family, friends, and community.

We will achieve our vision by treating all residents with honesty, integrity, respect, fairness and compassion. We will make our decisions with consistency, education, and common sense.
Association Goals
  • Preserve the beauty, value and enjoyment of Woodbridge by maintaining/improving common areas/facilities and enforcing our governing documents.
  • Ensure a healthy financial status with a sustainable Operations Budget and fully funded Capital Reserve.
  • Promote a safe, healthy, and harmonious environment that encourages mutual respect and open communications among members.
  • Listen and implement members’ suggestions, as appropriate, that improve or advance cohesiveness and enjoyment of our community.
  • Provide members opportunities to participate in Woodbridge Owners Association’s many social, sporting, volunteer, and leadership activities.
